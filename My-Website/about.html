About Me

Marinette Dupain-Cheng is a teenage girl from Paris who dreams of becoming a fashion designer while living a normal life with her family.
She secretly transforms into the superhero Ladybug, using magical earrings to fight villains and protect the city. Kind, creative, and brave, Marinette balances her everyday challenges with her responsibilities as a hero, making her an inspiring character.
